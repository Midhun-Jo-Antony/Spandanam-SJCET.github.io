About Us

“Only a life lived for others is a life worthwhile.”
~ Albert Einstein


Spandanam is an organisation that aims to improve the lives of people around us. All our volunteers are students of St.Joseph's College of Engineering and Technology,Palai. The team is led by Mr.Amal Sebastian and the staff co-ordinator is Mr.Babu Shankar, Asst.Prof.,SJCET Palai.

Uniform Collection

“If you wait until you can do everything for everybody, instead of something for somebody, you’ll end up not doing nothing for nobody.” ~ Malcom Bane.

Donate your old uniforms or any other clothes to Spandanam and we will make sure it reaches the person who needs it the most. Deposit your clothes at the collection baskets located in your department and be a part of this great initiative.

The uniform collection drive organised by Spandanam has concluded succesfully and we would like to thank everyone for their kind support extended during this venture, it encourages us to conduct more events in the future.
